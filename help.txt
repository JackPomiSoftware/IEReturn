IE Return allows you to enable Internet Explorer in newer Windows 10 and Windows 11 versions. However, the variety of options may confuse user, so here are the instructions:

- For Windows 10 1507-21H1
    1. Turn off BHO in BHO Options.
    2. Launch IE as an .exe file.

- For Windows 11 and Windows 10 21H2 and later
    1. Launch IE as a .vbs file.
